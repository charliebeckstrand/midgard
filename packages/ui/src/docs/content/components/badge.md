# Badge

A compact chip for labelling something inline — a status, a count, a category tag.

Badges ride alongside other content — a table row, a heading, a list item — to add a small, glanceable signal without taking a line of their own. Like a Button, a Badge is polymorphic and can become a link, and it can carry a small icon beside its label. Keep a badge's meaning in its text, not its color alone, so the signal survives for readers who can't rely on hue.
