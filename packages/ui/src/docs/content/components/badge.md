# Badge

A compact chip for labelling something inline — a status, a count, a category tag.

## Overview

Badges ride alongside other content — a table row, a heading, a list item — to add a small, glanceable signal without taking a line of their own. Like a Button, a Badge is polymorphic and can become a link, and it can carry a small icon beside its label.

## Usage

Reach for a Badge to annotate, not to act — a status beside a title, a count on a tab. Keep its meaning in the text, not the color alone, so the signal survives for readers who can't rely on hue.
