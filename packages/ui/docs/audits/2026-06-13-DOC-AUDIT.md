# Documentation Audit — `packages/ui`

**Date:** 2026-06-13 · **Scope:** the **public API surface** — every symbol a package barrel re-exports: each component plus its `*Props` / option / data types, `ui/hooks`, `ui/primitives/*`, `ui/providers/*`, `ui/core`, `ui/types`, and the internal `utilities/` and recipe-engine (`core/recipe`) surfaces. **Method:** manual source sweep against the TSDoc / JSDoc standard (pulled from the documentation knowledge base — `tsdoc` *syntax* / *tags*, `jsdoc` *reference*), delegated one subsystem per subagent. **All edits are comment-only — no code, types, imports, JSX, or formatting were changed.** Non-exported internal helpers, recipe-layer bodies (kiso / katakana / kata beyond the engine header docs), and self-evident prop fields were deliberately out of scope (see *Deferred*).

This audit accompanies the curated surface docs introduced in the same pass — [`COMPONENTS.md`](../COMPONENTS.md), [`HOOKS.md`](../HOOKS.md), [`PRIMITIVES.md`](../PRIMITIVES.md), [`PROVIDERS.md`](../PROVIDERS.md), [`RECIPES.md`](../RECIPES.md), [`CORE.md`](../CORE.md), [`UTILITIES.md`](../UTILITIES.md) — and the standard codified in [`CONVENTIONS.md`](../../../../CONVENTIONS.md) §12.

---

## Executive summary

The public surface was **already documented to a high bar before this pass** — most main components and nearly every hook carried answer-first, `@remarks`-rich doccomments at the quality of the `Button` / `useControllable` gold standard. The audit was therefore a *closing pass*, not a backfill: it filled residual gaps and tightened weak spots rather than writing from scratch.

The gaps clustered into a handful of repeatable patterns, not scattered one-offs:

1. **Exported types without a summary.** Many `*Props`, option, and data-shape types had documented *fields* but no statement of what the type *is* (`DateInputProps`, `SwitchProps`, `TextProps`, `GlassProviderProps`, `AnnounceOptions`, the Form binding-result types, the Sheet/Sidebar slot prop aliases, …). Filled with one-line summaries.
2. **Compound sub-components with documented fields but no symbol summary.** Whole families were thorough at the prop level yet bare at the declaration — `kanban` cards/columns/header, `list` item/label/description/handle, `command-palette` slots, `stepper-separator` / `stepper-panels`, the `sidebar` sub-parts, the `drawer` slot family, several `stat` parts. Each got an answer-first summary.
3. **Object/tuple-returning hooks missing `@returns`.** 14 hooks (mostly the floating, sortable, and a11y families) described what they do but not the shape they hand back; `@returns` added.
4. **Non-standard default/voice nits.** Prose "Defaults to X" and `@default` normalized to `@defaultValue`; a few summaries that led with the SSR/RSC caveat were rewritten answer-first (the caveat demoted to `@remarks`).
5. **Thin main-component summaries.** A minority of components (`drawer`, `command-palette`, `control`, `editable-grid`, `json-tree`, `kanban`, `list`, `legend`, `glass`) had one-liners that didn't capture load-bearing behavior; lifted to the gold standard with `@remarks` for the non-obvious parts (focus-trap precedence, live-region announcements, density cascades, WCAG specifics).

No public-surface symbol is left without at least a summary.

## Standard adopted

Codified in [`CONVENTIONS.md`](../../../../CONVENTIONS.md) §12. In short: summary-first first sentence; `@param` / `@returns` where the signature isn't self-evident; `@defaultValue` on defaulted optional fields; `@remarks` for caveats (SSR / server-client boundary, effect timing, a11y); `@see {@link …}` to cross-link; `@internal` on a documented non-exported helper; no restating the type, no documenting self-evident fields. House voice per [`CLAUDE.md`](../../../../CLAUDE.md) §2.

## Deferred (out of scope this pass)

These are real but lower-leverage and were intentionally left; record them here so a future sweep can pick them up:

- **Non-exported internal hooks** not on a barrel — e.g. `useAccordion`, `useAddressInputSuggestions`, component-private `use-*` seams. Documented opportunistically only.
- **Inline, non-exported prop shapes** (`OverlayProps`, `SelectTriggerProps`, some `sidebar` slot aliases) that never reach a barrel.
- **Recipe-layer bodies** — `kiso` / `katakana` / `kata` modules beyond the engine header docs. The layer is internal and already carries per-file `Layer: …` docblocks and rich READMEs; per-token TSDoc was not pursued.
- **Self-evident prop fields** — the standard controllable triad (`value` / `defaultValue` / `onValueChange`) and `ComponentPropsWithoutRef` pass-throughs were left uncommented by design (§12: don't document the obvious).
- **`@packageDocumentation` barrel blocks** were not added; the curated docs serve that role.
